Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PocketLevel ("the App"), operated by Kelotech ("we", "us"), handles your information.

Summary

PocketLevel is designed to work without collecting personal data. Measurements, calibration data, and saved readings are stored only on your device. The App contains no advertising and no third-party analytics SDKs.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or any measurement data. The camera feed used by measurement tools is processed entirely on your device and is never captured, stored, or transmitted by us.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. To manage subscription entitlements we use RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and purchase receipt information. RevenueCat does not receive your name or contact details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Device permissions

The App requests camera access solely to display live camera-based measurement tools. Motion and sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope, barometer) are read locally to compute level and angle readings. None of this data leaves your device.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
