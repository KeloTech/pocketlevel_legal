Support & FAQ

Need help with PocketLevel? Check the FAQ below or email us at info@kelotech.dev. We aim to reply within 2 business days.

My readings seem slightly off. What should I do?

Run Calibrate: place your phone on a known flat surface (or against a vertical reference) and follow the two-step calibration. Remove thick phone cases — the camera bump and cases can tilt the phone slightly, which is the most common cause of inaccurate readings.

How accurate is PocketLevel?

After calibration, level and angle readings are accurate to roughly a tenth of a degree, which is sufficient for hanging pictures, shelves, furniture, and everyday DIY. Distance readings are estimates. PocketLevel is not a replacement for professional instruments in structural or safety-critical work.

How do I cancel my subscription or free trial?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: open Settings on your iPhone → your name → Subscriptions → PocketLevel → Cancel. To avoid being charged after the free trial, cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

I subscribed before but the app asks me to pay again

Tap Restore on the paywall. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I get a refund?

Purchases are processed by Apple, and refunds are handled through Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Does PocketLevel work offline?

Yes. All tools work fully offline — no internet connection is needed for measuring.

Which devices are supported?

PocketLevel supports iPhones running a recent version of iOS. Some tools use the camera; all measurement processing happens on your device.
